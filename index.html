About the Event

Hack 'in' Thon 2025 is a collaborative hackathon hosted by the Department of CSE in partnership with GDG and proudly sponsored by Adya.ai. The event challenges students to design and implement impactful solutions for real-world campus issues using the Adya.ai platform. Teams of 3–4 will compete in two evaluation rounds for innovation, creativity, and technical execution.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can our team change members after registration?

No, team members or the team leader cannot be changed after registration. Once registered, the team composition must remain the same.

Is there a specific format for the Idea Submission?

Yes, the idea must be submitted in a PowerPoint presentation (PPT) with a maximum of 7 slides. You can download the PPT template from the website.

What are the key evaluation criteria for the idea submission?

The evaluation will focus on the originality of the idea, its alignment with SDGs, technical complexity, feasibility, and potential for meaningful impact. Additionally, user experience and future development opportunities will be considered.

May I use the Adya.ai platform in Round 1?

It’s not compulsory for Round 1, but you will need to use the Adya.ai platform in Round 2 for sure.
